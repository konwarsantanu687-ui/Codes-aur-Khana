Why Web Developers & New Founders Don't Make Money

Tutorial hell, over-engineering, aur selling skills na seekhna—sabse bade reasons jis wajah se real coding knowledge hone ke baad bhi log zero revenue par atke rehte hain.

1. The Over-Engineering Trap

New developers aur founders sabse badi galti ye karte hain ki wo product ko market mein launch karne se pehle use bekar ke complex features aur frameworks se load kar dete hain. Unhe lagta hai ki Next.js, Redux, multi-cloud clusters setup karne se revenue aayega. Reality ye hai ki user ko sirf functional clean tools chahiye jo unka kaam aasan karein. Jab tak product build hota hai, founder ka motivation khatam ho jata hai.

2. Zero Focus on Distribution & Selling

Aap duniya ka sabse behtareen system code kar lijiye, par agar aap use sell nahi kar sakte ya un target creators/workers tak nahi pahuncha sakte, toh aapki business valuation zero hai. Developers har roz naye languages seekhne mein time lagate hain, par 1 ghanta bhi direct marketing ya consumer acquisition par focus nahi karte. Business bina user conversion ke survive nahi karta.

⚠️ CRITICAL STRATEGY LEAKS:

• Tutorial Hell: Har din naya component tutorial dekhna par real independent functional products launch na karna.

• Ignoring Core Monetization: Product mein payment systems ya business baseline strategy pehle din se integrated na rakhna.

3. Falling For "Hype" Frameworks

Har dusre din social media par naye tech stacks promote kiye jaate hain. Developers product ka baseline architecture stable karne ke bajaye lagatar codes rewrite karte hain taaki wo 'cool' lag sakein. Asli money drive tab hoti hai jab aap plain logic, solid direct conversions (jaise HamaraMarket configurations), aur client interfaces par straight command bana kar launch karte hain.
