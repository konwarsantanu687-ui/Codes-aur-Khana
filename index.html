Day 01: The Ethical Mindset

Ethical hacking ki shuruat tool se nahi, balki mindset se hoti hai. Pehle din aapko ye samajhna zaroori hai ki ek system kaise kaam karta hai, taaki aap use secure kar sakein.

⚖️ Legal Awareness (Information Technology Act):
Hacking bina permission ke India mein Section 66 ke tehat illegal hai. Hamesha apni lab mein ya authorized environment mein hi practice karein.

Apne environment ko scan karne ke liye pehla basic command jo har hacker ko pata hona chahiye:

# Nmap scan to check open ports
nmap -sV -p 1-65535 localhost

// Output should show your local services
